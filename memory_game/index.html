Instructions

Concentration, also known as Match, Memory, Pelmanism, Shinkei-suijaku, Pexeso or simply
Paris, is a card game in which all of the cards are laid face down on a surface and two cards are
flipped face up over each turn. The object of the game is to turn over paris of matching cards.
